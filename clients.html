von Trapp Brewing

A little over a decade ago, Johannes von Trapp contemplated a brewery at his family’s hotel and resort in Stowe, Vermont. von Trapp Brewing is now an innovative brewery and 150-seat "Bierhall". The Rolec brew house system will eventually produce 50,000 barrels of beer annually - including von Trapp Brewing's award-winning Bohemian Pilsner.

Details

  • 3 mil BOPP
  • HD-7 Series
  • Expanded Color Gamut Technology


In addition to partnering with DWS on their bottle labels, von Trapp Brewing was one of our first Craft Brew clients to incorporate shrink sleeves on beer cans.


Visit von Trapp Brewing

Califia Farms



The name comes from the Spanish legend of warrior queen Califia who ruled over the mythical island of California. Spanish conquistadors believed they had discovered Califia’s bountiful island when they first explored California.

Califia Farms (pronounced like California) is one today’s fastest growing and most innovative beverage companies and we couldn’t be more proud to be partnering with them. With a varying product line in citrus juice, plant based almondmilks, and cold brew coffees, Califia tastes as good as it looks.

Gold's Horseradish

DWS prints more Gold's Horseradish labels than any other pressure sensitive label.

One of DWS' longest-running clients, it was Gold's that was the catalyst for helping DWS expand into flexographic label printing in 2009. DWS and Gold's share common roots (pun intended) in that they both operated out of Brooklyn, NY for decades, and both are 4th generation family owned and operated, Long Island-based manufacturers.

Details

  • 3 mil BOPP film

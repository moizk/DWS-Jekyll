Brooklyn Brewery

One of DWS' fastest growing craft brewing partners, Brooklyn Brewery continues to push the envelope with super innovative labels that include metallized papers, embossing patterns and unique "out of the box" designs.

Fun Fact

DWS and Brooklyn Brewery share some common roots in that the brewery, located at 79 N. 11th Street is located only blocks away from where DWS operated from 1920 through 1965 on Berry and S. 5th Street. Come on down to the brewery and have a pint or two. Better yet, have a bottle or two. (the bottles have those cool labels on them, after all...)

Label Specs

Lager, Hand & Seal & Local 1- printed on silver metallized paper using UV-cured PMS inks. Blast - printed on white semi-gloss with custom UV-cured PMS inks.

Health Ade

Health Ade took Kombucha to an entirely new level with its recent rebranding initiative. Together, the Health Ade and DWS teams worked tirelessly to execute a fresh, new look that not only looks great but feeeeels great in your hand with its silky smooth matte lamination. Even if you're not a kombucha lover (yet), you will be once you give Health Ade a try.

Details

Health Ade has more than doubled its sales since the release of its new labels.

Designed with the craft beer and natural & organic markets in mind and made from 100% Post-Consumer Recycled fiber, DWS Printing proudly introduces Legacy Label®

This 51 lb. Wet-Strength label paper is manufactured carbon neutral using 100% renewable electricity through a combination of direct low-impact hydroelectric and purchased wind power energy credits. Legacy Label paper provides a more natural look and feel than conventional gloss coated labels, while its bright whiteness still ensures accurate color representation on press.

Craft brewers and those in the natural & organic channels are committed to making sure their packaging has minimal impact on the environment and our Legacy Label® gives them a new way to take that commitment to the next level. While certain clients have successfully 'greened' their packaging by using post-consumer content in their secondary packaging, primary product labels have traditionally been made using only virgin fiber. Whether you're creating an organic product line or simply wish to reflect a sense of eco-consciousness, Legacy Label® is the natural choice.

Legacy Label Paper offers the following qualities:

  1. 100% Post Consumer Recycled
  2. Wet- Strength
  3. Manufactured Carbon Neutral
  4. Manufactured Process Chlorine Free
  5. Manufactured Using On-Site Low Impact Hydroelectric Power
  6. Manufactured From Responsibly Managed Forests
  7. Manufactured Green-E Certified Clean Renewable Wind Power

Benjamin H. Owens III is an artist.

He is also DWS' Creative Director and our favorite secret weapon. A graduate of R.I.T with a Dual Degree in Illustration and Art History, Ben sees the world differently, making him a rare partner in brand design. His vision is often the catalyst that can transform a label into an invitation to try the experience within that glass bottle.

Much of this insight is derived from his seemingly endless reserves of energy. Always creating, he is a former gilder & carver for the world famous Sotheby's auction house and his paintings have been featured in Juxtapoz Magazine and collected by people such as: Peter Max and Mark Ryden. Ben is also the National Award Winner for the Pabst Blue Ribbon Contest and has been garnered the title of Long Island's VIP Artist by Pulse Magazine - no big deal.

We love being able to offer his talent as a resource to our clients - from labels, to cartons, to point of sale materials, we can help you create an experience that will compel customers and convey your unique story.
